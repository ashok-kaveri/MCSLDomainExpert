# How to configure carrier account in the Shopify Multi Carrier Shipping Label app?

Shipping is one of the most tedious and frustrating parts of an eCommerce business. This becomes more hectic if you are using multiple carriers to handle shipping. If you’re manually handling the shipping process from the carrier website, the process becomes even more time-consuming. You need to login to different carrier accounts, enter the customer details for each order, and then print the shipping label. As your business grows, the fulfillment process with multiple carriers becomes even more complicated.

The easiest and the best solution to this would be the [**Shopify Multi Carrier Shipping Label app**](https://apps.shopify.com/multi-carrier-shipping-label). So, we will see how the app seamlessly integrates with multiple carriers and how to configure different shipping carriers in the app.

With the app, you can integrate multiple carriers like USPS, FedEx, UPS, DHL, Canada Post, Australia Post, and much more at the same time and automate the shipping process. You can use your own carrier account with negotiated rates and then display shipping rates at checkout, print shipping labels in bulk, and automate the tracking process by sending tracking notifications to the customers via email.

So, let’s see how to integrate different carrier accounts with the app and the process of shipping with these carriers.

## USPS Shipping account integration with Shopify

USPS is one of the most widely used shipping carriers. The app helps in completely automating USPS shipping with Shopify by allowing merchants to display real-time USPS shipping rates on the Shopify checkout page (provided, [**carrier-calculated rates**](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/shipping/setting-up-and-managing-your-shipping/enabling-shipping-carriers) is enabled on your Shopify store), print USPS shipping labels in bulk from within Shopify using your own USPS account, and enable Shopify USPS tracking for all the orders.

You can enable USPS shipping with the app either by directly using your **USPS account** or by using the **stamps.com account**.

#### **Shopify USPS shipping with Stamps.com account**

With the [**Stamps.com account**](https://print.stamps.com/SignIn/), you can display real-time USPS rates on Shopify checkout and also print the USPS shipping labels. In order to configure the Stamps.com account with the app, you can use the same Username and Password that is used for the Stamps.com account login, as shown below.

Stamps.com offers a simple and most convenient way to print USPS labels. As a Stamps.com user, you’ll get discounts of up to 40% off on certain USPS services. 

> **Note** : As an **[authorized partner of Stamps.com](https://www.stamps.com/shipping/integrations/pluginhive/)** , we would be able to help you seamlessly integrate Shopify with Stamps.com, in case you face any issues. 

#### **Shopify USPS Shipping with USPS e-VS account**

As an alternative to the Stamps.com account, you can also use your own USPS account to handle the shipping process. You can get the credentials from your USPS account which can be used to configure in the app.

But, in order to print USPS labels using this account, you have to [**enable e-VS for your USPS account**](https://www.usps.com/postalone/applyevs.htm). But, you can still show live USPS rates at checkout without enabling e-VS.

For more information regarding automating USPS shipping with Shopify, visit [**Shopify USPS shipping**](https://www.pluginhive.com/product/shopify-usps-shipping-app-rates-shipping-labels-tracking/).

## UPS Shipping account integration with Shopify

The app helps in completely automating UPS shipping with Shopify. It allows merchants to display real-time UPS shipping rates at Shopify checkout page (provided, carrier calculated rates is enabled on your Shopify store), use UPS negotiated rates from your own account, print UPS shipping labels in bulk from within Shopify, and enable Shopify UPS tracking for all the orders.

To configure your UPS account with the app, you need the following credentials.

1\. Account number  
2\. User ID  
3\. Password  
4\. Access Key

[**Login to your UPS account**](https://id.ups.com/u/login/identifier?state=hKFo2SBzaTgyVlpZY3lfNTQxWkJVaDg2d0tjcVp0R3R6RzJ6V6Fur3VuaXZlcnNhbC1sb2dpbqN0aWTZIERISlV1OFJaWHh5bWJCLTY3V01TVEtyTXVyeE5OenNpo2NpZNkgaHdMRVROQnNxTWNzemtmRVA5RVNHaEZZMjI1YTlvZmM&ui_locales=en). User ID and Password are the same that you use for the UPS account login.

Under the account dashboard, head to “Payment Options” from where you will find the UPS account number. You can then request for the “access key” that will be sent to your email as shown below.

For more information regarding automating UPS shipping with Shopify, visit [**Shopify UPS shipping**](https://www.pluginhive.com/product/shopify-ups-shipping-app-rates-shipping-labels-tracking/).

## FedEx Shipping account integration with Shopify

The app helps in automating FedEx shipping with Shopify by allowing merchants to display live FedEx shipping rates at Shopify checkout (only if carrier-calculated rates feature is enabled on your Shopify store), print FedEx shipping labels in bulk from within Shopify, and automate Shopify FedEx tracking for all the orders by sending tracking status emails to customers via email.

To configure your FedEx account with the app, you need the following credentials.

1\. Account number  
2\. Meter Number  
3\. Web Services Key  
4\. Web Services Password

[**Login to your FedEx account**](https://www.fedex.com/en-us/developer.html). Head to the “FedEx Web Services” and select “Develop and Test”. Now, select “Get your test key”, fill up all the details and continue to the next page. On this page, you will get the Account number, Meter number, and Web Services Key. The Web Services Password will be sent to you via email.

## DHL Shipping account integration with Shopify

The app helps in automating DHL express as well as DHL Packet shipping with Shopify. It allows merchants to display real-time DHL shipping rates at Shopify checkout page (provided, carrier calculated rates is enabled on your Shopify store), print DHL shipping labels in bulk from within Shopify, and enable Shopify DHL tracking for all the orders.

To configure your DHL Express account with the app, you need the following credentials.

1\. Account number  
2\. Site ID  
3\. Site Password

[**Login to your DHL XML portal**](https://xmlportal.dhl.com/login). Head to “XML services status” under the account dashboard. Here, you will find the Account number and Site ID. The Site Password will be sent to you via email, as shown below.

Similar to DHL express shipping, the app also supports DHL Packet shipping. To configure the DHL Packet account, you require the Account number, User ID, and Password. You also need to provide the Bank details which are needed for COD orders. After collecting the Order Amount from the customer, DHL Packet will transfer the collected money to the account.

For more information regarding automating DHL shipping with Shopify, visit [**Shopify DHL shipping**](https://www.pluginhive.com/product/shopify-dhl-shipping-app-rates-shipping-labels-tracking/).

## Canada Post Shipping account integration with Shopify

The app helps in fully automating Canada Post shipping with Shopify by allowing merchants to display real-time Canada Post shipping rates at Shopify checkout (if carrier calculated rates are enabled on your Shopify store), print Canada Post shipping labels in bulk from within Shopify, and automate Shopify Canada Post tracking for all the orders.

To configure your Canada Post account with the app, you need the following credentials.

1\. API Keys User Name  
2\. API Keys Password  
3\. Customer Number  
4\. Service Type  
5\. Contract Number

[**Login to your Canada Post account**](https://www.canadapost.ca/cpo/mc/business/productsservices/developers/services/gettingstarted.jsf). Under this section, you will get the **Customer Number** and **Contract Number**. Now, under the API Keys, you will get the API Keys User Name and API Keys Password as shown below.

For more information regarding automating Canada Post shipping with Shopify, visit [**Shopify Canada Post shipping**](https://www.pluginhive.com/product/shopify-canada-post-shipping-app-rates-shipping-labels-tracking/).
