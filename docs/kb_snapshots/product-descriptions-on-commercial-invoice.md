# Product Descriptions on Commercial Invoice for Easy Customs Clearance

Product Descriptions on the commercial invoice reduces the risk of your shipments being held at customs and saves you from paying more duties and taxes than you should.

Here are some of the ways you can display accurate customs product descriptions in Commercial Invoices within your eCommerce store.

* * *

## On this page

  1. Product Descriptions for Easy to Understand Product Names
  2. Product Descriptions for the Same Category of Products
  3. Declare the Product Descriptions for Each Product
  4. Products Description for Large Sets of Products

* * *

## 1\. Product Descriptions for Easy to Understand Product Names 

If your product names are easily understandable for Customs, for example, Blue Leather Watch, THFlex Blue Plaid Suit, Green tea moisturizer, and likewise, you are good to go printing your Commercial Invoices. By default, the [PH MultiCarrier Shipping Label app](https://www.pluginhive.com/shopify-multi-carrier-shipping-label-app/) displays the exact Product Names on the Commercial Invoice.

* * *

## 2\. Product Descriptions for the Same Category of Products 

If you are selling the same category of products, like Electronic Gadgets, using the app you can add customs descriptions for instance “electronic goods” for all your products.

To add Customs Descriptions for all the products head to:  
  
**Settings > General Settings > Shipping > Customs Description to be displayed in the Commercial Invoice **

* * *

* * *

## 3\. Declare the Product Descriptions for Each Product

Your catchy product titles like Marvel Coffee Mug or Gold Candle Stand can create misunderstandings during customs clearance. However, proper customs descriptions like Marvel Printed Coffee Mug- Merchandise or Candle Stand- Color Gold can save from misleads & confusion. 

To declare the customs descriptions for each product:

  * Head over to your **Products** and then select a product for adding the custom description as shown below:

* * *

* * *

  * Scroll down to **Customs > Edit Customs > Add Customs Descriptions**

* * *

* * *

  * By default in the [PH MultiCarrier Shipping Label app](https://www.pluginhive.com/shopify-multi-carrier-shipping-label-app/), the Customs Description will be the **Product Name**. To change it to **Product Description** head over to:  
  
**Settings > General Settings > Shipping > Customs Description to be displayed in the Commercial Invoice > Product Description**

## 4\. Products Description for Large Sets of Products

If you want to add Customs Descriptions for multiple products you can export all your products, add descriptions & import them using the app:

  * **Products > Export CSV**
