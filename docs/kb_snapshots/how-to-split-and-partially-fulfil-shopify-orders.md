# How to Split and Partially Fulfil Shopify Orders

In many real-world situations, merchants need to ship part of an order while holding the rest, due to backorders, pre-orders, or longer processing times. Partial fulfilment helps manage this, but handling it efficiently can be challenging without the right tools.

The **[PH Multi Carrier Shipping Label app](https://apps.shopify.com/multi-carrier-shipping-label?)** simplifies this process by integrating directly with Shopify. You can:

  * Generate labels and fulfil only the ready-to-ship items first.
  * Later, print labels and fulfil the remaining items once they’re available.

This setup ensures faster deliveries, better customer experience, and complete control over multi-stage shipments. Here’s how to split Shopify orders and fulfil them using the **[PH Multi Carrier Shipping Label app](https://apps.shopify.com/multi-carrier-shipping-label?)** , ensuring a smooth and trackable process.

## 1\. Split the orders in Shopify

When a customer’s order includes multiple items, and not all of them are ready to ship, Shopify lets you split the order into multiple shipments. This way, you can fulfil the available items immediately while keeping the remaining ones pending for future fulfilment.

Here’s how you can split the order(s) by putting them on hold:

  * Go to your **Shopify admin > Orders** and click on the order with the unready items

* * *

  * In the **Unfulfilled** section, click the three-dot menu (⋯) and choose **“Hold fulfillment”**.

* * *

  * Select the item(s) you want to put on hold and choose a reason.

* * *

  * These product(s) will be excluded from the current fulfilment cycle but remain within the order for future processing.

* * *

## 2\. Fulfilling Ready-to-Ship Items First

Once items are on hold in Shopify, only the ready-to-ship items appear in the PH Multi Carrier Shipping Label app. You can now process and ship these available items.

  * Go to the order in the **Orders** tab. Confirm item details, **carrier** , **service** , **packaging** , and **address**. You can **edit** any of this before continuing.[ ](https://chatgpt.com/c/686b9671-04cc-800d-adbd-f1389754f691#)Refer to this guide for details on [**editing order information**](https://www.pluginhive.com/knowledge-base/setting-up-shopify-multi-carrier-shipping-label-app/).
  * Click **Generate Label** to create **shipping labels** for the ready items.

* * *

  * Click **Print Documents** to get the shipping labels for your orders. The app will automatically redirect you to print the shipping labels. To print the other documents, like the tax invoice, packing slip, picklist, etc, select **Print Documents > All** option.

* * *

  * Once ready for shipping, **request a pickup** within your **Shopify store** by selecting the order and clicking **Request Pickup**.

* * *

  * After the request, check the **Pickup** tab to view pickup status and other details.

* * *

  * Go back to the **Orders** tab, select the same order, and click **Mark As Fulfilled**.

* * *

  * **Shopify** automatically updates the **order status**. Check the **Shopify > Orders** page to confirm **tracking info** and **fulfilment status**.

* * *

  * Click on the order to see that the products shipped using the app are automatically**marked as** **fulfilled** in Shopify, while the other product(s) still remain on hold.

* * *

  * The **app** sends **tracking details** to the customer via the **Shopify Order Shipped Email**.

* * *

## 3\. Fulfilling the Remaining Items Once Ready

Once the **on hold products** are available, begin the next stage of order fulfillment.

### Release Items from On Hold Status

First, you’ll need to release the held items in Shopify to make them available for shipping.

  * Go to the original order in **Shopify > Orders**. Find the items under the **On Hold** section and click **Release hold**.

* * *

  * Shopify updates the order status to **Partially Fulfilled**.

* * *

  * Go to the **PH Multi Carrier Shipping Label app** and click the **Refresh** icon next to the order.

  * Open the order. The **released items** now appear in the **order details** and are ready to process.

* * *

### Fulfil the Remaining Products

You can now generate labels and fulfil the order to complete the second shipment.

  * In the app, open the order. Only the remaining items that were previously on hold appear in the order summary page. 

* * *

  * Review all **shipping details**. Click **Generate Label** or use **Generate Label and Fulfill** to handle both steps in one click.

* * *

  * Click **Print Document** to print the **shipping label** and related documents (if required).

For details on scheduling a **pickup** , **fulfilling** the order, and **tracking** the shipment, you can refer to the steps outlined earlier in this article.

* * *

**Note:**   
The **Shopify Order Timeline** always displays the tracking details and fulfillment history for all items that have been shipped. You can refer back to this timeline at any time to review past tracking information.   
  
Additionally, tracking details can be confirmed within the app’s **Tracking** tab or in the **tracking history section** at the bottom of the order details page.
