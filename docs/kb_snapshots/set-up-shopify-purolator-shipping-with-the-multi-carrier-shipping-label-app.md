### Configure the weight & dimensions for your products and choose the right packaging method

To calculate the accurate shipping rate for an order, it is very important that you configure the weight of the product correctly.

To set the weight and dimensions for your products, head to the **“Product”** section in the app.

#### **Add the product weight and dimensions**

In most cases, only weight is required to handle the packaging and calculate the accurate rates. So, by default, the app will calculate the rates based on the **weight-based packaging method**.

However, if the volumetric weight of your product is higher, you can add the dimensions of the product and [**choose an advanced packing method**](https://www.pluginhive.com/knowledge-base/setting-up-shopify-multi-carrier-shipping-label-app/#advanced-packing-methods).

#### **Weight-based packing**

In this packing method, you can set a **maximum weight (max.weight)** for your box and the app automatically packs the maximum number of products in this box based on the max.weight.

Now, to configure the weight-based packaging method, head to **App Settings -- > Shipping --> Packaging**. You can configure the max.weight of the box and the app will automatically handle the packaging based on that. If required, you can configure the **box weight** and also add a **max.quantity** that will be packed in a box.

* * *

**_Reference_**  
  
For More Details on Weight-based packing, Read – [Shopify Product Weight & Dimensions](https://www.pluginhive.com/knowledge-base/setting-up-shopify-multi-carrier-shipping-label-app/#configure-weight-dimension).   

* * *

However, if the volumetric weight of your product is higher, you can add the dimensions of the product and choose an advanced packing method.

The app allows you to choose from some of the most advanced packaging methods to efficiently pack your products into the boxes and save shipping costs. These packing methods include:

  * **Box Packing**  
A packing method based on both the weight & dimensions, where you can create your own boxes with custom weight & dimensions for your products. The app will automatically calculate the correct box size that fits your products and save you a lot of hassle.
  * **Stack Packing**  
An advanced packing method based on the total height of the products, where the app calculates the correct box suitable for your products by stacking up all the products to a certain height.
  * **Quantity Based Packing**  
Using this method, you can set up the packing based on the number of items that can be packed in one box. You can add a box based on your product dimensions and you can set the **max** **quantity** that can be packed in one box.

For More Details on Configuring custom Shipping Boxes Read – [Shopify Advanced Packaging Methods](https://www.pluginhive.com/knowledge-base/setting-up-shopify-multi-carrier-shipping-label-app/#advanced-packing-methods).

### Purolator Special Services

#### Purolator Signature Types

Purolator offers several types of signatures or delivery confirmations for its shipping services, depending on the specific needs of the shipper and the recipient.

**Adult Signature Required**  
Enabling this signature means it will require the recipient to provide proof of age through a government-issued photo ID at the time of delivery. This option is used for packages containing age-restricted items such as alcohol, tobacco, or other products that require an adult’s signature to receive.

**Origin Signature not Required (OSNR)**  
This service allows shippers to send packages without requiring a signature upon delivery. It is an ideal option for businesses that are shipping to residential addresses or to recipients who may not be available to sign for their packages. 

**Residential**  
When a package is sent using Purolator Residential Signature, the recipient must sign for the package upon delivery. The signature is collected by the Purolator courier and serves as proof of delivery. This service is ideal for businesses that are shipping high-value or sensitive items to residential addresses. 

**Chain Of Signature**  
When a package is sent using the Purolator Chain of Signature, every person who handles the package along the delivery route is required to sign for it. This includes the Purolator courier who picks up the package, any sorting or distribution center personnel, and the final recipient. Each signature in the chain is recorded and can be viewed online through Purolator’s tracking system, providing shippers with a complete chain of custody for their package. It is an ideal option for businesses that are shipping high-value or sensitive items that require an additional level of security and accountability.

### Frequently Asked Questions

Here are the answers to some of the frequently asked questions to help you understand this app better.

#### a. I see there is a mismatch in rates while comparing the rates shown in the app and my account rates with the carrier. What can be the reason? 

The rates mismatch could happen due to incorrect or missing configurations in the Ship From Address, Ship To Address, Weight, Dimensions, Package Type, or the Carrier Services & Rates. 

* * *

**_Reference_**  
For More Details on How to Fix Rates Mismatch Read – [Inaccurate Shipping Rates at Shopify Checkout](https://www.pluginhive.com/knowledge-base/troubleshooting-shopify-multi-carrier-shipping-label-app/#rates-mismatch).   

* * *

#### b. I ship around 50-100 orders per day. Generating labels one by one is not feasible for my business. Does this app help automate the process for me in any way?

Absolutely!

The app can automate the process of generating shipping labels as soon as your orders come in. Based on the shipping services selected by the customers, the app will generate the labels automatically. 

All you need to do is select the orders and print 100s of labels in a single click.

* * *

#### c. We ship from multiple warehouses. Will this app help me set up my shipping from multiple warehouses?

Yes! The app allows you to set up multiple addresses for your warehouses. Based on your business requirements, the app allows you to create different automation rules.

While printing shipping documents like Purolator shipping labels, Commercial Invoices, Tax Invoices, Return Labels, etc. the app allows you to customize the print layout as well as choose the number of copies that you want to print.

All you need to do is visit **Settings > General Settings > Print Settings** and modify the document print layout and the number of copies, as shown below.
