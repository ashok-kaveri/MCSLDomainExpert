# Open a Free UPS Account and Get Exclusive Shipping Discounts in Europe

This guide will walk you through the process of opening a free UPS account through PluginHive, specifically for Shopify merchants. By following these steps, you will be able to **take advantage of up to 75% off all your shipping costs**.

To take advantage of these discounted rates:

  * Install the PluginHive Shopify Shipping App
  * Open a free UPS account through PluginHive & get 75% shipping discounts

* * *

## **Install the PluginHive Shopify Shipping App:**

To install the app, follow these steps:

From the dashboard of your Shopify store, navigate to the Apps section and search for **“PluginHive”** in the Shopify app store.

* * *

Once you’ve located the app, click “**Add app** ” and then “**Install** ” to start the installation process.

* * *

After the installation is complete, select a **“Price plan”** and get started.

* * *

The app will automatically configure your store location as the shipping origin. It is important to verify this address as it will be used for calculating the shipping rates and scheduling pickups. With that, the installation is now complete.

* * *

## **Open a free UPS account:**

To open a **Free UPS account** and receive shipping discounts, please follow these instructions:

Click on “**Start** “

* * *

In the carrier section, select **“UPS (OAuth)”**

* * *

To take advantage of this offer, select the “**Sign Up** ” option and proceed

* * *

**_Note_**  
  

If you’re not from the UK, France, Netherlands, Italy, Spain, Germany, or Belgium, please refer to this guide: “[Setting up Shopify Multi-Carrier Shipping Label App](https://www.pluginhive.com/knowledge-base/shopify-ups-shipping/)“  
  
Or, watch this YouTube Video: “[How to integrate UPS with Shopify](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=1Ju3OxA37Qs\])“

* * *

The UPS Registration page will open. Fill in all the required details. 

  1. **Title:** This is a prefix used before a person’s name to signify their gender or marital status. It’s commonly “Mr.” for men, “Mrs.” for married women, and “Ms.” for women regardless of marital status.
  2. **First name and Last name:** These are self-explanatory. Provide your full legal name.
  3. **Preferred username:** This is the name you’d like to use as your login identifier for UPS services. It should be something unique and easy to remember.
  4. **Password:** A strong password should contain a mix of uppercase and lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters. Make sure it’s something you can remember but difficult for others to guess.
  5. **Company name:** If you’re representing a business or organization, enter the full legal name of your company.
  6. **Email:** Provide an active email address where you’ll receive notifications, updates, and communications from UPS.
  7. **Phone number:** UPS may need to contact you via phone for various reasons. Enter your phone number, excluding the country code.
  8. **Billing Address:** This is the complete address associated with your payment method. It’s important to enter it accurately to ensure proper billing and delivery of invoices.

* * *

Once done, click on **“CREATE PROFILE.”** after entering all the details correctly.

* * *

Now, click on “**CONNECT TO YOUR UPS ACCOUNT** “

* * *

The **Login** page will open where you’ll be prompted to input the UPS username or email you provided during registration. Ensure to review and agree to the terms of the agreement. Once done, click on the “**Continue** “.

* * *

Enter your **UPS Password** and click on **Log In**.

* * *

The UPS Technology Agreement page will open. Please read the agreement carefully, and once you’ve done so, **accept the terms and conditions**. Then, click on “**Next**.”

* * *

If your pickup address is the same as the billing address, you can go ahead and click on “**CREATE SHIPPER ACCOUNT** ” to complete the registration process. 

* * *

**_Note_**  
  

**Tax ID** and **Tax ID Type** fields are optional in the registration form but are mandatory for certain countries like Spain.

* * *

* * *

If your pickup address is not the same as the billing address, please fill in the pickup address in the designated fields and then click on “**CREATE SHIPPER ACCOUNT** ” to complete the registration process.

* * *

The License Agreement page will open. Please read the document and **accept the terms and conditions**. Then, click on “**APPLY PROMO CODE** “.

* * *

Here, you will be able to see the **“Registration Successful”** message as shown below:

* * *

Now that your UPS account has been successfully created and linked to our Shopify shipping App, you can start shipping with UPS.

* * *

Upon successful registration, the UPS team will **send you a confirmation email** that includes your account details. You will receive a PIN, which you will use to add your account to your ups.com profile, giving you the ability to pay bills online and manage your account settings.

* * *

Once you have a free UPS account linked to your PluginHive Shopify Shipping App, you can start shipping your products at discounted rates. To further improve your experience with UPS, we recommend focusing on two key aspects of your shipping journey: **Billing** and **Claims**.

**For billing** , UPS has implemented a new solution through [ups.com](https://www.ups.com/gb/en/Home.page) called the UPS Billing Center. This self-serve portal enables customers to view, analyze, pay, and dispute their UPS invoices. Customers that have had their account opened via PluginHive can self-enroll for Billing Center with the following steps:

  * Go to [UPS.com](https://www.ups.com/gb/en/Home.page)
  * Select “View and Pay Bill” from the “My Profile” menu
  * Select “UPS Billing Centre” from the “Tools and Resources” menu
  * Select “Enrol in the UPS Billing Centre” and follow the instructions
  * The [UPS.com](https://www.ups.com/gb/en/Home.page) ID you just created grants you access to applications on [UPS.com](https://www.ups.com/gb/en/Home.page), including the UPS Billing Centre

To enroll, the customer will need to go through invoice verification using an invoice no older than 45 days. Check out this YouTube video to learn more about the “[UPS Billing Center](https://youtu.be/mb4KWWgfXWU)” and the value it brings.

**For claims,** we recommend that you know what to do in case of any issues that may arise. Here is a link to the [ups.com](https://www.ups.com/gb/en/Home.page) UK page where this topic is explained: “[File a Claim | UPS – United Kingdom](https://www.ups.com/gb/en/support/file-a-claim.page)“

*_You can find the same information on your country’s[ups.com](http://ups.com/) page by selecting your country and language._
